Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Just use the sign-up form at the top of the page. Signing up is free.
How does this work?
You can sign up for our lecture series. All lectures will be made available via Zoom Webinar. You will receive a weekly schedule by email and an email reminder one hour before each webinar.
Will I get a degree/a certificate?

There is no degree or academic certificate for attending Legal Tech Essentials, as this is a lecture series and not an academic program and there is no final exam. However, this year, we will offer a PDF Attendance Record through our executive education branch, Bucerius Education GmbH, for those of you who need it (e.g. to submit for CLE credits). Please note that the attendance record will be provided at the price of EUR 175 to offset the costs involved. It will be issued only after the program has ended. To be able to track attendance it is of vital importance that you use the same email address for all Zoom meetings and when purchasing the attendance record. We cannot guarantee any form of recognition by your bar association/similar for CLE credits. You can purchase the attendance record here.

Do I need books or additional materials?
No. If you want to get a taste, check out our intro session and our playlist of Legal Technology Lectures at Bucerius Law School.
Do I need to attend all lectures?
No. You can pick and choose. We will upload a schedule here, email you a weekly schedule update and you will get a reminder 1 hour before each lecture.
Will there be recordings of the sessions?
Recordings for most sessions will be available to participants for the duration of the program. For each session, it is up to the individual lecturer if they want their talk to be recorded. As the recordings need to be edited and we want to encourage live participation, recordings will be available with a delay of one week.

Still have a question? Send us an email at essentials@law-school.de.
