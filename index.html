Welcome Back

Legal Tech Essentials 2022

When we started Legal Tech Essentials in 2020, little did we dream that over 8,500 people would take part in our program. In 2021, we built on our experiences from the first iteration.

This year, we are back with a new program that brings exciting content to even more timezones:

For the first time, Legal Tech Essentials will be a joint effort this year between Bucerius Law School’s Center for Legal Technology and Data Science and Singapore Management University’s Centre for Computational Law at the Yong Pung How School of Law.

From mid-November to early December, we are going to host inspirational presentations from some of the world's leading Legal Technology and Operations experts Tuesdays, Wednesdays, and Thursdays at 2:30 p.m. Hamburg Time / 8:30 p.m. Singapore Time / 6:30 a.m. Central Time. We promise 60–90 minutes of insights and Q&A.

Be sure to sign up in time to receive updates on topics, lecturers and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Just use the sign-up form at the top of the page. Signing up is free.
How does this work?
You can sign up for our lecture series. All lectures will be made available via Zoom Webinar. You will receive a weekly schedule by email and an email reminder one hour before each webinar.
Will I get a degree/a certificate?

There is no degree or academic certificate for attending Legal Tech Essentials, as this is a lecture series and not an academic program and there is no final exam. The attendance record will not be offered again this year.

Do I need books or additional materials?
No. If you want to get a taste, check out last year's intro session and our playlist of Legal Technology Lectures at Bucerius Law School.
Do I need to attend all lectures?
No. You can pick and choose. We will upload a schedule here, email you a weekly schedule update and you will get a reminder 1 hour before each lecture.

Still have a question? Send us an email at essentials@law-school.de.
