Legal Tech Essentials 2024

Legal Tech Essentials has been around for a while, but recently things have gone a little quiet around us. But that is about to change...

This year, we are building momentum and bring back our tested partnerhsip between Bucerius Law School’s Center for Legal Technology and Data Science and Singapore Management University’s Centre for Computational Law at the Yong Pung How School of Law.

This November, we are going to host inspirational presentations from some of the world's leading Legal Tech, Operations and AI experts starting with an intense three lectures on November 13, 14 and 15 at 7:00 p.m. Hamburg Time / 1:00 am Singapore Time / 1:00 p.m. Eastern Time followed by weekly lectures at 8:00 p.m. Singapore Time / 1:00 p.m. Hamburg Time / 7:00 a.m. Eastern Time until early December. As usual, we promise 60–90 minutes of insights and Q&A.

Be sure to sign up in time to receive updates on topics, lecturers and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up?
Just use the sign-up form at the top of the page. Signing up is free.
How does this work?
You can sign up for our lecture series. All lectures will be made available via Zoom Webinar. You will receive a weekly schedule by email and an email reminder one hour before each webinar.
Will I get a degree/a certificate?

There is no degree or academic certificate for attending Legal Tech Essentials, as this is a lecture series and not an academic program and there is no final exam and no attendance record.

Do I need books or additional materials?
No. If you want to get a taste, check out our last intro session and our playlist of Legal Technology Lectures at Bucerius Law School.
Do I need to attend all lectures?
No. You can pick and choose. We will upload a schedule here, email you a weekly schedule update and you will get a reminder 1 hour before each lecture.
